Welcome to Sunrise

Two decades of trusted healthcare in Warangal

Since 2004, Sunrise Multispecialty Hospital has grown from a 30-bed clinic into a 300-bed multispecialty centre, combining advanced medical technology with a genuinely patient-first approach. Our teams in cardiology, orthopedics, neurology, women's health and critical care work together so patients get coordinated, timely treatment.
